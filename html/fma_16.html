Condensed milk

Description

Manufacturer's Stocks of evaporated and sweetened condensed milk.

Usage

condmilk

Format

Time series data

Source

Makridakis, Wheelwright and Hyndman (1998) Forecasting: methods and applications, John Wiley & Sons: New York. Exercise 7.5.

Examples
