funcharts: Functional Control Charts

Description

Provides functional control charts for statistical process monitoring of functional data, using the methods of Capezza et al. (2020) doi:10.1002/asmb.2507, Centofanti et al. (2021) doi:10.1080/00401706.2020.1753581, and Capezza et al. (2024) doi:10.1080/00401706.2024.2327346. The package is thoroughly illustrated in the paper of Capezza et al (2023) doi:10.1080/00224065.2023.2219012.
