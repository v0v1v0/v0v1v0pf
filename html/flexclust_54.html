dentitio R Documentation

Dentition of Mammals

Description

Mammal's teeth divided into the 4 groups: incisors, canines, premolars and molars.

Usage

data(dentitio)

Format

A data frame with 66 observations on the following 8 variables.

top.inc

Top incisors.

bot.inc

Bottom incisors.

top.can

Top canines.

bot.can

Bottom canines.

top.pre

Top premolars.

bot.pre

Bottom premolars.

top.mol

Top molars.

bot.mol

Bottom molars.

References

John A. Hartigan: Clustering Algorithms. Wiley, New York, 1975.
